Winging it on Wheels

Doing a bit of a cycle so thought I would throw together a website for anyone interested.

The plan is in September 2024 to leave my comfortable job, pack my life into panniers, my bike into a box and begin the cycle of a lifetime.

I start in Vancouver (or maybe Seattle) and cycle south with the goal to make it to Ushuaia, Argentina. Have I mentioned that I have never bikepacked before? Or wild camped. Don't know Spanish. Am wildly out of shape and have never even changed a tire or fixed a puncture. I am wildly unprepared.

Anyway, thanks for visiting. Most info will be in tabs along the top if you are interested.
